Ancient Nepal

The history of Nepal begins with the Kirat Dynasty, followed by the rise of the Licchavis and Mallas.

Medieval Nepal

The Malla Kings ruled the Kathmandu Valley, fostering art, culture, and trade.

Modern Nepal

The Shah Dynasty unified Nepal, and the Rana regime controlled the country for over a century.

Recent Developments

Nepal transitioned to democracy, abolished the monarchy, and became a federal republic in 2008.
